Projects:

Fortune Queller

Step into the ethereal realm as The Oracle, a gifted fortune teller who saw a glimpse of their future. After witnessing an impending doom, you must enter the Spirit Realm, armed with tarot cards, and battle with the spirits that threaten your fate.

30 Minutes or It's Free

A fast-paced, adrenaline-fueled action-platformer where you play as an overconfident, thrill-seeking pizza delivery guy on a mission to deliver a fresh, hot pizza to the boss at the top of a massive corporate skyscraper. The catch? You've got just 30 minutes to get there!

Diggy Dog

In Diggy Dog, you play as an adventurous dog named Diggy, searching for the world-renowned Idol of Gringus Khan. You must push crates, dodge moles, and collect keys to get closer to the treasure in this puzzle-platformer game!

Toilet Time Traveler

This WarioWare-inspired game will have you traveling across time to sort trading ships, block incoming arrows, and even close annoying pop up ads! You'll have endless amounts of fun in this endless microgame game!
